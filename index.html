Wasabi White Chocolate Truffles

A fusion of sweet and spicy, these truffles feature a creamy white chocolate ganache infused with a hint of wasabi, coated in a layer of rich dark chocolate and dusted with matcha powder for a vibrant finish.

Balsamic Strawberry Basil Tart

A modern twist on a classic dessert, this tart features a buttery shortbread crust filled with tangy balsamic-marinated strawberries and fresh basil leaves, topped with a dollop of whipped mascarpone cream.

Saffron Rosewater Rice Pudding

Fragrant and exotic, this rice pudding is made with aromatic saffron-infused rice cooked in creamy coconut milk and sweetened with rosewater, garnished with pistachios and edible rose petals for a luxurious touch.

Charcoal Black Sesame Ice Cream Sandwiches

Bold and striking, these ice cream sandwiches feature black sesame seed ice cream sandwiched between two charcoal-infused cookies, creating a visually stunning dessert with a nutty, earthy flavor profile.
